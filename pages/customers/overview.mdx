## Managing Your Users: The Customers API

The Customers API allows you to efficiently manage the individuals or businesses using your platform. With it, you can create, retrieve, update, and maintain comprehensive customer records — all in one place.

### Core Features

- **Create Customers**  
  Seamlessly register new users with essential information to simplify the onboarding process.

- **List All Customers**  
  Retrieve a complete list of registered customers for reporting, analytics, or internal management.

- **Retrieve a Single Customer**  
  Fetch detailed data for a specific customer using their unique ID.

- **Update Customer Records**  
  Modify existing user details — such as name, email, or phone number — to keep your database accurate and up to date.

### Advanced Use Cases

- **Personalized Payments**  
  Customize payment flows based on individual user preferences or profiles.

- **Transaction History Tracking**  
  Associate transactions with customer profiles for deeper purchase insights.

- **Enhanced Customer Support**  
  Quickly access relevant customer data to resolve issues faster and more effectively.

- **Targeted Marketing**  
  (With proper consent) segment your customers for personalized, high-conversion campaigns.

- **Regulatory Compliance**  
  Maintain clean, auditable customer records to meet industry standards and compliance requirements.
