## Authentication

### Overview

Get started with Sayswitch! Our comprehensive payment APIs enable you to process transactions across multiple currencies and regions using various payment methods. Follow these steps to begin:

### Step 1: Register Your Account
Begin by signing up for your complimentary Sayswitch developer account. Upon registration, you'll immediately gain access to our sandbox environment for testing and development.

### Step 2: Verify Your Business Identity
To activate live transaction processing, we require verification of your business credentials. Submit your company documentation and business information for our compliance review to ensure optimal service delivery.

### Step 3: Launch Your Payment Integration
After successful verification and approval, you're ready to process real customer transactions. Our support team is available to assist you throughout the integration process.
