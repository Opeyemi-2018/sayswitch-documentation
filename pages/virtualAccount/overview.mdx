## Virtual Accounts

Virtual Accounts empower businesses by assigning unique bank account numbers to individual customers. This approach enhances the efficiency of fund collection, simplifies payment tracking, and enables seamless automatic reconciliation.

### Key Features:

1. **Create Virtual Account**  
   Easily generate a dedicated virtual bank account for each customer, allowing precise tracking of incoming payments.

2. **Retrieve Virtual Account Details**  
   Access detailed information about any specific reserved virtual account, including account number, status, and associated customer data.

3. **List All Virtual Accounts**  
   Retrieve a comprehensive list of all virtual accounts reserved under your business, enabling better management and monitoring of customer payments.

By leveraging virtual accounts, businesses can improve cash flow management, reduce reconciliation errors, and provide customers with a more transparent payment experience.
