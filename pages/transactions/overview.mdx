### Transfers Introduction

SaySwitch provides a robust and secure way to transfer funds between accounts. Our transfer solutions are designed to be seamless and efficient, ensuring that your transactions are processed quickly and securely.

### Transfer Features

SaySwitch provides robust APIs to manage money transfers efficiently. Below are the key transfer-related features available:

1. **Initialize Transfer**  
   This feature allows you to initiate a fund transfer from your wallet to a beneficiary account. You'll need to provide essential details such as the amount, recipient account information, and a unique reference to track the transaction.

2. **Fetch Transaction**  
   Retrieve detailed information about a specific transaction using its reference or ID. This is helpful for confirming transaction status, validating payment history, or debugging failed transfers.

3. **Transaction Timeline**  
   View the chronological timeline of events related to a particular transaction. This includes timestamps for when the transfer was initiated, processed, and completed. It provides transparency and helps with audit trails.

4. **Transaction Total**  
   This endpoint aggregates your transactions to return summarized metrics, such as the total number of transfers and total amount sent within a specific timeframe. It's useful for reporting and reconciliation purposes.
